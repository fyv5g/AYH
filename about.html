Who We Are

Established in 2022, AYH Car Dealership has swiftly become a trusted name in the automotive industry, with its roots deeply embedded in Malawian soil.

"Dedicated to quality and service, our expansion into Botswana and the establishment of our headquarters in Durham, South Africa, mark key milestones in our commitment to serve the Southern African market."

With a focus on reliability and customer satisfaction, AYH continues to expand its services and offerings, ensuring a top-notch experience for every client. Our ethos is to deliver excellence across every aspect of our business, from sales to support.
